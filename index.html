Evaluation Methodology

How we conducted an unbiased, comprehensive evaluation of 8 AI platforms using real trading questions.

Our Approach

1
Same Questions, Same Time
All 8 AIs received similar or identical questions asked within the same time period. No AI had access to different information or context. Each question was designed to test real trader needs, not theoretical scenarios.
2
Blind Evaluation
Responses were evaluated based on objective criteria: comprehension, actionability, originality, accuracy, and risk awareness. The evaluator did not know which AI produced which response during scoring to prevent bias.
3
Real Trading Scenarios
9 questions covering market outlook, risk analysis, scenario planning, and tactical trading decisions. Each question represents actual situations traders face daily, ensuring practical relevance.
4
Transparent Scoring
Each response received a 0-100 score based on weighted criteria: 25% comprehension, 25% actionability, 20% originality, 20% accuracy, and 10% risk awareness. All raw responses are available for review.

Ensuring Unbiased Evaluation

No Favoritism
All AIs evaluated using identical standards. Free and paid services judged by the same criteria. No special treatment for any platform.
Objective Criteria
Scoring based on measurable factors: specific numbers vs. vague statements, actionable insights vs. generic advice, original frameworks vs. template responses.
Full Transparency
Every response, score, strength, and weakness is documented. You can review all raw AI outputs to verify our assessments independently.
Critical Errors Tracked
Wrong data, dangerous advice, or comprehension failures result in severe score penalties. These are explicitly flagged for transparency.

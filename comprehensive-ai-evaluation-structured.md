## METHODOLOGY

### Scoring Scale
- **0-20**: Useless or dangerous - Wrong data, comprehension failures, or advice that could lose money
- **21-49**: Below average - Generic, lazy, or unhelpful
- **50**: Average - Not good, not bad, just forgettable
- **51-79**: Good range - Useful analysis with real value
- **80-99**: Excellent - Original insights, actionable intelligence
- **100**: Perfect - Impossible to achieve

### Evaluation Criteria
1. **Comprehension** (25%) - Did they understand what was asked?
2. **Actionability** (25%) - Can a trader use this to make money or avoid losses?
3. **Originality** (20%) - Unique insights vs. generic aggregation?
4. **Accuracy** (20%) - Are numbers, facts, and logic correct?
5. **Risk Awareness** (10%) - Do they warn about what could go wrong?
